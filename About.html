Scientia Beverage Consulting provides a comprehensive offering of marketing, customer experience, and strategy services to breweries, meaderies, cideries, and other companies in the beverage industry.

Founder and Chief Consultant

Matt Johnson, Founder and Owner, is experienced in the execution of strategy and business projects across many industries. He has been in the craft beverage industry for multiple years working in Sales, Operations, and Brewing. Matt is certified by the Cicerone Certification Program, Beer Judge Certification Program, and multiple project management / business organizations. He has judged many brewing competitions and is Vice President of the MN Homebrewers Association.

Need help getting your brewery off the ground? Need advice on how to best take your product to market or how to take your company to the next level? Reach out to discuss what we can do for you.
