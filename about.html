Scientia Beverage Consulting provides a comprehensive offering of marketing, customer experience, and strategy services to breweries, meaderies, cideries, and other companies in the beverage industry. Started as a passion project and interest in helping local breweries advance and sustain growth, Scientia has grown into a company that can make an impact quickly on any size brewery.

Founder and Chief Consultant

Matt Johnson, Founder and Owner, is experienced in the execution of strategy and business projects across many industries. He has been in the craft beverage industry for multiple years working in Sales, Operations, and Brewing. Matt is certified by the Cicerone Certification Program, Beer Judge Certification Program, and multiple project management / business organizations. He has judged many brewing competitions and is President of the MN Homebrewers Association.

Leveraging years of experience in Business Consulting combined with formal training / certifications, learnings through working at breweries, and a passion for the craft & community, he can take breweries from an idea to reality or accelerate their growth to reach the 'next level'.
